How to Link GBP Products to City Pages

  1. Edit Profile on Google Business Profile
  2. Go to Services or Products section
  3. For each service, add a custom link to the corresponding city page
  4. Example: "Bounce House Rental (Phoenix)" links to www.yoursite.com/bounce-house-rental-phoenix

On-Page NAP Match: Critical for Rankings

Your city landing page MUST include:

  • Business Name - Character-identical to GBP
  • Address - Full address, character-identical
  • Phone - Same format as GBP
  • Place all three in the footer or contact section
  • Mention the city throughout the page (not just "Phoenix" once, but multiple times in context)

JSON-LD Schema Markup (The Secret Weapon)

Embed this structured data on every city landing page (in your <head>):

<script type="application/ld+json">
{
  "@context": "https://schema.org",
  "@type": "LocalBusiness",
  "name": "Bounce House Paradise",
  "address": "123 Main St, Phoenix, AZ 85001",
  "telephone": "(480) 555-1234",
  "areaServed": "Phoenix, AZ",
  "service": ["Bounce House Rental", "Water Slide Rental", "Obstacle Course Rental"],
  "hours": "Monday-Friday 9am-6pm"
}
</script>

This tells Google's AI: "This business serves Phoenix. These are the services. This is the address. This is the phone." Perfect data for AI Overview extraction.

  • Service descriptions with city keywords
