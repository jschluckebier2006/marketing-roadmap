The Complete Marketing Roadmap for Bounce House & Party Rental Business Owners

Presented by Jamie Schluckebier | KO Marketing Agency | Wave Con 2026

Stop Relying on a Single Marketing Channel

Most bounce house operators make the same mistake: they pick one or two marketing channels and call it a day. Maybe you post on Facebook Marketplace and wait for the phone to ring. Or you set up a Google Business Profile and hope for the best. But here's the truth — the most successful rental businesses don't depend on a single channel. They build a funnel.

What is a marketing channel?

Each "stream" flowing into the funnel above represents a marketing channel — a distinct path that potential customers take to discover your business. Google Ads, SEO, Facebook Marketplace, Instagram, word of mouth, community flyers, vehicle wraps — these are all separate channels. The more channels you activate, the wider the top of your funnel gets, and the more rental bookings flow out the bottom.

Think about it: if Facebook Marketplace is your only channel and the algorithm changes tomorrow, or your account gets restricted, your entire lead flow dries up overnight. We've seen it happen to operators over and over.

The businesses that are consistently booked solid — the ones pulling in $200K, $500K, even $1M+ — have multiple channels feeding their funnel at all times. When one slows down, the others pick up the slack. That's how you build a business that doesn't keep you up at night.
