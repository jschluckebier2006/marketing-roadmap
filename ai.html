AI for Competitor Analysis

You don't need expensive tools or certifications to understand what your competitors are doing. AI can do the analysis for you in seconds.

What You Can Do:

  • Screenshot their website → paste into Claude → ask "What keywords is this site targeting? How can I outrank them?"
  • Ask for a full SEO audit: "Review this website for SEO issues. Check heading structure, keyword usage, mobile design, and content quality."
  • Direct comparison: "Compare my website to this competitor and give me 5 specific improvements I should make."
  • Analyze their services: "What services are they listing that I'm not offering? Which ones should I add?"
  • Study their ads: Visit Google Ads Transparency Center to see what ads competitors are running, then ask AI to reverse-engineer their strategy
Example Prompt:

"I've pasted a screenshot of my competitor's bounce house website. I'm also attaching my own website. Compare them side by side and tell me: (1) What keywords are they targeting? (2) How is their page structured? (3) What are their main value propositions? (4) How can I outrank them? (5) What specific changes should I make to my website?"

AI for Website Audits

You don't need to hire an expensive SEO consultant. Use AI to audit your own website for free.

Things You Can Ask AI:

Homepage Optimization

"How can I improve this page for conversions? Is the headline compelling? Do I have a clear CTA?"

SEO Audit

"Review this page for SEO issues. Check heading structure, keyword usage, content quality, and CTA placement."

Headline Improvement

"Rewrite my homepage headline to be more compelling for parents searching for bounce house rentals in my area."

Mobile Design

"Review how this page looks on mobile. Is the text readable? Are buttons easy to tap? Is the layout optimized?"

Trust Signals

"What trust signals am I missing? Should I add reviews, testimonials, guarantees, or certifications?"

Content Gap Analysis

"What information should I add to this page to answer all customer questions and improve ranking?"

AI for Content Creation

This is where AI really shines. Write entire blog posts, landing pages, and service descriptions in minutes.

Blog Post Generation

Prompt: "Write a 1,500-word blog post titled 'The Ultimate Guide to Planning a Bounce House Birthday Party in [City], GA' Include sections about choosing the right bounce house, budgeting, themes, add-on services, indoor vs outdoor options, and party planning tips. Make it SEO-optimized for keywords like 'bounce house rental [city]' and 'birthday party ideas.'"

City-Specific Landing Pages

Prompt: "Write a unique 800-word landing page for bounce house rentals in [City], GA. Include local landmarks, schools, popular event venues, and neighborhoods. Optimize for keywords like 'bounce house rental [city]' and 'party rentals in [city].' Include a strong headline, pain point section, service overview, testimonials placeholder, and CTA."

Service Descriptions

Prompt: "Write 10 Google Business Profile service descriptions (exactly 300 characters max) for a bounce house rental company. Include keywords for: bounce house rental, water slide rental, foam party, mechanical bull, photo booth, dunk tank, party planning, corporate events, and same-day rental. Each should be unique and compelling."

Review Responses

Prompt: "Write professional, keyword-rich responses to these Google reviews for a bounce house rental company in [City]. Include the service name when relevant and add local keywords: [paste reviews here]."

AI for Ad Copy

AI can write multiple variations of ad copy in seconds. Perfect for A/B testing Google Ads, Facebook Ads, and more.

Google Ads Headlines & Descriptions

Prompt: "Write 5 Google Ads headlines (max 30 characters each) for a bounce house rental company in [City]. Each should highlight a different benefit: price, speed, variety, kids' smiles, or safety. Then write 3 Google Ads descriptions (max 90 characters each) highlighting free delivery, same-day availability, and 20+ year experience."

Facebook Ads

Prompt: "Write 3 Facebook ad copies (50-80 words each) targeting parents planning birthday parties in [City]. One should focus on fun, one on convenience (same-day booking), and one on safety/quality. Include a sense of urgency and a clear CTA."

Email Subject Lines

Prompt: "Write 5 email subject lines for a bounce house company promoting a 'Book Early, Save 20%' summer special. Make them attention-grabbing but not spammy."

AI for Email Marketing

Build entire email sequences that nurture leads and drive bookings.

Follow-Up Sequences

Prompt: "Write a 3-email follow-up sequence for customers who just booked a bounce house rental but haven't provided payment yet. Email 1: Confirm booking and outline next steps. Email 2 (sent 2 days later): Friendly reminder about payment. Email 3 (sent 5 days later): Final reminder with payment options and incentive (free table rental, etc.)."

Seasonal Promotions

Prompt: "Write a promotional email for summer water slide rentals. Target busy parents planning last-minute birthday parties. Include benefits, a special offer, and urgency. Keep it to 150 words."

Referral Requests

Prompt: "Write a referral request email for existing bounce house customers. Don't sound pushy or salesy. Focus on helping their friends make kids' parties amazing. Include referral incentives (free add-on service or $20 off for both parties)."

AI for Business Strategy

Get strategic advice on everything from pricing to service expansion without paying a consultant.

Service Expansion

Prompt: "I own a bounce house rental company in a mid-sized market (population 150,000). I've been in business for 3 years and currently generate $250k in annual revenue. What new services should I add to increase revenue by 30-50%? Consider my current customer base, market demand, startup costs, and profit margins."

Pricing Strategy

Prompt: "What's the best pricing strategy for party rentals in a small/medium market? Should I compete on price, offer premium bundles, or go niche? What are typical profit margins? How should I price foam parties vs bounce houses?"

Marketing Calendar

Prompt: "Create a quarterly marketing calendar for a bounce house rental company. Include content ideas, promotional campaigns, email sequences, social media themes, and seasonal opportunities. Cover March through October."

Pro Tips for Getting Better AI Results

  • Be Specific: Include your city, services, target audience, and business context
  • Give Context: "I run a bounce house rental company in [City] serving families with kids ages 4-12 and corporate event planners"
  • Ask for Revisions: "Make this more conversational" or "Add more keywords" or "Make this shorter"
  • Use it Iteratively: Start broad, then refine. AI gets better with follow-up prompts
  • Provide Examples: If you like a competitor's ad copy, paste it and say "Write something in this style"
  • More Detail = Better Output: The more specific you are, the better the AI results
  • Edit & Customize: AI is a starting point. Edit, customize, and make it yours
